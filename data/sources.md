# Data Sources

This document tracks all data sources used in the Right-Wing Tracker project.

## Politics & Elections

### Voting Laws
- **Source**: Brennan Center for Justice - Voting Laws Roundup
- **URL**: https://www.brennancenter.org/our-work/research-reports/voting-laws-roundup
- **Update Frequency**: Quarterly
- **Confidence**: High

### Election Interference
- **Source**: Department of Justice - Election Crimes
- **URL**: https://www.justice.gov/criminal/criminal-vns/criminal/vns/criminal/vns/election-crimes
- **Update Frequency**: As events occur
- **Confidence**: High

### Oversight Erosion
- **Source**: Congressional Research Service reports
- **URL**: https://crsreports.congress.gov
- **Update Frequency**: As events occur
- **Confidence**: High

## Media & Information

### Journalist Arrests
- **Source**: Committee to Protect Journalists (CPJ)
- **URL**: https://cpj.org/data/
- **Update Frequency**: Monthly
- **Confidence**: High

### SLAPP Suits
- **Source**: First Amendment Coalition
- **URL**: https://firstamendmentcoalition.org/
- **Update Frequency**: As cases filed
- **Confidence**: Medium

### FOIA Denials
- **Source**: FOIA.gov
- **URL**: https://www.foia.gov/data.html
- **Update Frequency**: Annual
- **Confidence**: High
